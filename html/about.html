About Us

Who We Are

At Skin Algnosis, we are transforming the future of skin health using cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our mission is to support both individuals and healthcare professionals in the early detection and analysis of skin lesions, including potentially cancerous conditions like melanoma. We aim to make skin diagnostics faster, more accurate, and accessible — anytime, anywhere.

Scope of AI Diagnosis

Our AI diagnosis system is currently limited to a defined set of skin diseases due to constraints in dataset availability and medical labeling. The model is trained only on certain conditions, and it is not capable of detecting all types of skin abnormalities.

Supported Skin Diseases:

  • Melanoma
  • Melanocytic nevus
  • Basal cell carcinoma
  • Actinic keratosis
  • Benign keratosis
  • Dermatofibroma
  • Vascular lesion
  • Squamous cell carcinoma

The system performs best on clear, high-quality images taken under good lighting with minimal obstructions. Results may be less accurate for blurry, shadowed, or uncommon variations of lesions. For optimal results, please ensure images are well-lit and focused.

We are continuously working on expanding the database and improving accuracy to support a broader range of skin conditions in the future.
